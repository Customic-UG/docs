The **Osiro MCP server** lets you drive Osiro from Claude or any AI assistant that supports connectors. Once connected, you can ask your assistant to build a funnel, restyle it, dig into its analytics, or draft and launch an email sequence — all in natural language, without leaving the chat.

Everything runs as **you**: the assistant only ever sees the funnels, messages, and analytics in the organizations your Osiro account belongs to.

## Connect the server

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add the connector">
    In Claude (or your MCP-capable assistant), add a new connector and paste the Osiro MCP URL:

    ```text
    https://api.osiro.app/mcp
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    You'll be sent to Osiro to log in and approve the connection. This links the assistant to your Osiro account securely — you can revoke access at any time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start asking">
    That's it. Ask your assistant to list your funnels, check last week's numbers, or draft an email sequence.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  The assistant always works on your funnel's **draft**. Reviewing and publishing changes stays in your hands inside the Osiro app.
</Info>

## Key things to know

### Funnels

A funnel is a series of **screens** (steps). Each screen holds **blocks** — headings, text, inputs, buttons, choices, and more. Your assistant can create a funnel, add or reorder screens and blocks, group fields together, and set up what happens after each screen (auto-advance, branch to another screen, submit, or redirect).

Basic funnels work with no setup — a single-choice question advances on its own, and a button submits or moves forward. You only need to describe custom logic when you want branching.

### Analytics

- A **start** is when someone opens your funnel; a **completion** is when they submit it.
- **Conversion rate** = completions ÷ starts, counted per visitor session.
- You can narrow any report to a **slice** — a single campaign, device type, browser, or referrer — to compare how different audiences perform.
- All dates and times are in **UTC**.

### Styling

Brand colors, element and form colors, heading/body fonts, corner radius, spacing, step animations, and full-width vs. boxed layout are all adjustable. Just describe what you want changed — text colors are picked automatically for good contrast, so you don't have to manage them.

<Note>
  If a funnel shares a style guide with others, restyling it automatically makes a private copy first — so your other funnels are never affected.
</Note>

### Messages & email sequences

- A **message** is an email you write in plain, simple formatting; Osiro themes it to look good automatically.
- A **sequence** drips messages to your leads over time, with waits in between (for example, "2 days" or "36 hours").
- You can restrict a sequence to a specific **audience**, and have it stop early when a lead converts.

<Warning>
  Sequences are always created **paused** — nothing is sent until you activate one. Activating requires a **verified email sender** on your organization, and begins real sends to real leads. Every email includes an unsubscribe link automatically.
</Warning>

## Privacy & access

- The assistant only sees data in the organizations **your** Osiro account can access.
- You sign in through Osiro to authorize the connection, and can revoke it any time from your account.
- Nothing is sent to your leads until you explicitly activate a sequence.

## Need help?

If a request doesn't work as expected, try asking your assistant to "list my funnels" or "list my organizations" first — it works best when it starts from your real data rather than guessing. For account, billing, or sender verification issues, reach out to the Osiro team through your dashboard.
